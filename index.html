Ask About Partner Support

What counts as a small update?

  • Changing text, pricing, hours, services, or contact details
  • Replacing photos or adding recent project images
  • Updating button links, payment links, or social links
  • Adding a short announcement, testimonial, or promo message
  • Checking forms and making sure customer requests are coming through

What is not included monthly?

  • Full redesigns, new websites, or unlimited revisions
  • New logos, full brand identity packages, or advanced graphics
  • Daily posting, ad management, complex e-commerce, or emergency same-day work
  • Large copywriting projects or new multi-page funnels
  • Major custom code features beyond the agreed plan
