ANUME Private Chef & Catering

ANUME needed a digital launch that felt as thoughtful and elevated as the private dining experiences behind the brand.

The Challenge

Building the right first impression

The business was moving beyond an early product-focused identity and needed its private chef, catering, curated tastings, and upscale experience to lead the story. Pricing also needed to remain consultation-based because every event is different.

Our Solution

A digital experience built around the business

We repositioned the website around premium experiences, introduced stronger service pathways, created a consultation-focused call to action, connected the new domain, and designed a rich visual experience that works across devices.

The Outcome

A stronger foundation for what comes next.

ANUME is now live with a polished, credible online presence that clearly presents the full business and gives prospective clients a direct path to plan an experience. The site can be expanded later with new menus, galleries, testimonials, and booking tools.
