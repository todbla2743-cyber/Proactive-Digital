Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

The short version: We collect the info you give us when you contact us or work with us. We use it to respond to you and deliver your project. We don't sell it, share it with advertisers, or do anything weird with it. If you want us to delete your data, just email us.

1. Who we are

Proactive Digital ("we," "us," "our") is a web design service based in Chesterfield, Virginia, operated by Todd Blackwell Jr. under TRB Family Business. Our website is getproactivedigital.com. You can reach us at hello@getproactivedigital.com.

2. What information we collect

Information you give us directly

When you fill out our contact form, email us, or book a call, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your business name (if provided)
  • Any message or project details you share
  • Which package you're interested in

Information collected automatically

Like most websites, we collect basic technical info when you visit:

  • IP address and approximate location
  • Browser and device type
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referrer (the link that sent you here)

This is standard analytics data that helps us understand how people find and use the site.

During a client project

If you hire us, we'll collect whatever information is needed to build your site: logos, photos, business content, payment details for invoicing, and any login credentials you share for third-party services we integrate. We only collect what the project requires.

3. How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiries. If you fill out a form, we use your info to reply, answer questions, and send quotes.
  • To deliver your project. For clients, we use your content and credentials to build and launch your site.
  • To send occasional updates. You may get follow-up emails about your project or future improvements. You can opt out any time.
  • To improve the site. Anonymous analytics help us understand what's working and what isn't.

We do not sell your data, rent your email list, or share your info with advertisers.

4. Tools we use

We rely on trusted third-party services to operate the business, which may include:

  • A website hosting and form-submission provider
  • Analytics tools for anonymous traffic data
  • A scheduling platform if you book a call with us
  • An email marketing platform if you opt in to our list
  • A payment processor for invoicing and client billing

These providers are selected for their security and privacy standards. Specific providers may change over time without affecting this policy. Contact us if you'd like to know which service handles a specific piece of your data.

5. Cookies

We use minimal cookies, mostly for analytics and remembering your theme preference (light/dark mode). You can block cookies in your browser settings. It won't break the site.

6. How long we keep your data

Contact form submissions stay in our hosting platform's dashboard indefinitely unless you ask us to delete them. Client project data is kept for as long as we're working with you, plus a reasonable archive period for reference. If you want anything deleted, just ask.

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Ask what information we have about you
  • Correct anything that's wrong
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of any marketing emails (every email has an unsubscribe link)

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@getproactivedigital.com and we'll handle it within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data, including HTTPS encryption, secure password practices, and working with reputable service providers. That said, no system is 100% secure. If we ever become aware of a data breach that affects you, we'll let you know promptly.

9. Children's privacy

This site isn't directed at children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect information from them. If you believe a child has sent us information, email us and we'll delete it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will tell you when. Significant changes get a notice on the homepage or by email if you're a client.
